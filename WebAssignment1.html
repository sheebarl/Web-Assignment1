About DreamWorld

A visit to Dream World gives you the chance of visiting an amusement park set in the backdrop of the biggest waterfalls of Kerala, Athirappilly and Vazhachal. This state-of-the-art facility boasts of over 42 rides, with more than half of them being pure water rides. Its 8ft deep Wave Pool along with a Raft Ride that is over 30 ft. high make it among the best amusement parks in the country.

Rides in Water Theme Park

Dream World have a number of other rides and attractions such as Baby Car, Caterpillar, Sky Train, Flying Colombus, Striking Car, Revolving Barrel, and Dragon Train. The water rides include Zoom Ride, Mat Racer, Dream Shower, Dream Splash, Space Bowl, Amazon River, and Crazy Cruise.
